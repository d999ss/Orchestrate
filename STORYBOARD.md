# Branded Matrix · Storyboard

**Project:** Orchestrate 2026 — GridOS Customer Conference
**Tagline:** A More Intelligent Grid · Connected. Intelligent. Global.
**Audience:** Utility operators, grid software customers, partners.
**Format:** 16:9 · ~60 sec · 27 frames (9 beats × 3 sub-shots)

## The story

A day in the life of an intelligent grid. We open at the smallest scale — a single arc of electricity at dawn — and pull all the way out to a planetary network of orchestration. Along the way the grid wakes up, brings on traditional and renewable generation, gets challenged by a storm, reroutes itself in real time using GridOS, and keeps the lights on for the people and places that depend on it. The film closes on the vision: a more intelligent grid, connected, intelligent, global.

## Visual treatment

- **Real footage + emerald data overlay.** Every shot lives at the intersection of real grid infrastructure and the brand's emerald-green data-viz language. Substations, transmission lines, wind turbines, control rooms — all overlaid with thin emerald network lines, halftone data textures, and bright cyan node points where the grid is "thinking."
- **Palette.** Deep teal/black background, emerald green for the grid overlay, vibrant cyan for active nodes/insights, lime `#A6FF00` reserved for the closing brand reveal.
- **Camera.** Anamorphic, broadcast-grade, deliberate. Locked or slow-tracking. No frantic motion.
- **No people on camera** until 7B (hospital/factory/school footage) — and even there, partial silhouettes only.
