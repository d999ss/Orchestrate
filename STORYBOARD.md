# Branded Matrix · Storyboard

**Project:** Orchestrate 2026 — GridOS Customer Conference
**Theme:** A More Intelligent Grid · Connected. Intelligent. Global.
**Format:** 16:9 · ~60 sec · 27 frames (9 beats × 3 sub-shots)

## Visual Direction

- **Palette.** Emerald green dominant (matching the conference promo). Lime `#A6FF00` reserved for brand reveals only. Black backgrounds, halftone dot textures, geometric mesh.
- **Aesthetic.** Data-network visualization. Wireframe geometry. Halftone-dot continents. No people in the abstract narrative — humanity appears as silhouette in Beat 6 only.
- **Motion.** Deliberate, not decorative. Camera moves only when it earns the cut. Energy flows along structure, not as ambient effect.
- **Audio bed.** Hybrid score, builds across Beats 1-5, hero moment at 6, sustains through 9.

## Beat 01 · The Beginning
*Absolute zero, then the first point.*

### 1A · Absolute void · 2.0s
- **Visual:** Black frame. No grain, no gradient.
- **Camera:** Locked.
- **Veo prompt:** *Pure black frame. Absolute void. No motion, no light, no texture. Hold for two seconds. Cinematic.*

### 1B · First data point · 2.0s
- **Visual:** A single emerald-green dot fades in dead center. Hard edge, no glow, perfectly still.
- **Camera:** Locked.
- **Veo prompt:** *A single small emerald-green pixel-like dot fades in at the exact center of a pure black frame. Hard edge, mathematically precise, no glow. Cinematic minimal.*

### 1C · Field emerges · 2.5s
- **Visual:** More emerald dots fade in around the first, forming a scattered halftone field. Density grows from zero.
- **Camera:** Locked.
- **Veo prompt:** *Field of emerald-green pixel dots emerging from black, forming a scattered halftone pattern. Dots fade in at varying densities like data points lighting up. Cinematic minimal data visualization.*
