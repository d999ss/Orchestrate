One Watt
From a turbine to a child's nightlight

Follow a single watt of electricity from the moment it leaves a wind turbine to the moment it powers a child's nightlight. In sixty seconds it travels through every piece of the grid: cable, substation, transformer, switching station, distribution line, meter, wall, outlet, bulb. Vision to value, told through the smallest possible unit.
